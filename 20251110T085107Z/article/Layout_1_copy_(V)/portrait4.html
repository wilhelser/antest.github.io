Eggnog pancakes

Turn that last bit of eggnog into a delicious

morning treat. Swap it for the milk in your

favorite pancake batter recipe, or try this one:

In a large bowl mix together 2 cups

all-purpose flower, 1 Tbsp. baking powder,

  1 ⁄ 2  tsp. salt, 1 ⁄ 4  tsp. ground nutmeg, 2 lightly

beaten eggs, 2 cups eggnog, and 1 ⁄ 4  cup oil.

Cook pancakes on a griddle 1 to 2

minutes or until golden brown.
